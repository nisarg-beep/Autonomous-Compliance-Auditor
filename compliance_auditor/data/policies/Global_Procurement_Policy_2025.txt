GLOBAL PROCUREMENT POLICY
Version: 2.1
Effective Date: Jan 1, 2025

PAYMENT TERMS
Standard payment terms for all vendors are Net 45 days from the receipt of a valid invoice.
Any terms exceeding 60 days require explicit written approval from the CFO.
Under no circumstances shall payment terms exceed 90 days.

LIABILITY AND INDEMNIFICATION
2.1 Liability Cap: The Vendor's liability shall not be limited to less than two times (2x) the total annual contract value or $1,000,000, whichever is greater.
2.2 Indemnification: Vendor agrees to indemnify the Company against any third-party claims arising from intellectual property infringement or data breaches.

GOVERNING LAW
All contracts shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of New York.
Arbitration clauses are permitted but must be conducted in New York City.

DATA PRIVACY
All vendors processing employee or customer data must be ISO 27001 certified.
Data must be encrypted at rest and in transit.
